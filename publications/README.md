## What Are Brahim Numbers?

Brahim Numbers are a newly discovered sequence of integers that emerge from a special mathematical expansion involving the golden ratio. What makes them extraordinary is that these same numbers appear independently in three completely different areas of mathematics and physics:

**The Sequence:** 27, 42, 60, 75, 97, 121, 136, 154, 172, 187, ...

These numbers follow a beautiful symmetry rule: any number in the sequence, when added to its "mirror partner," always equals 214. For example: 27 + 187 = 214, and 136 + 78 = 214.

For over a century, physicists have wondered: **Why do the fundamental constants of nature have the values they do?** Why is the fine structure constant approximately 1/137? Why not 1/100 or 1/200?
