### The Key Conflict: Why They Don't Unite

**General Relativity says:**
- Spacetime is smooth, continuous, dynamical
- Geometry IS gravity
- No quantum uncertainty in geometry

**Quantum Mechanics says:**
- Everything should be quantized
- Uncertainty principle applies universally
- Spacetime should fluctuate at Planck scale

**The Incompatibility:**
```
GR: smooth spacetime + massive objects → curvature
QM: quantum fields + interactions → particles

Combining them → infinities (non-renormalizable)
```
