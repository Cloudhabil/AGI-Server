## PART TWO: THE PROBLEM STRUCTURE

### RH in Mathematical Context

**The Statement:**
```
All non-trivial zeros of ζ(s) satisfy Re(ζ) = 1/2
OR equivalently:
All zeros of ζ(s) lie on the line Re(s) = 1/2
```

**Why it matters:**
- Fundamental to understanding prime distribution
- 10,000+ theorems depend on its truth
- Connects multiple mathematical fields
- Represents frontier of analytical capability

**Why it's hard:**
- Location is not forced by known constraints
- Mechanism creating location is unknown
- Infinity barrier blocks computational proof
- No consensus on what approach would work
