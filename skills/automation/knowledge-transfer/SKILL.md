# Knowledge Transfer

Use this skill to distill and hand off knowledge.

## Workflow

1) Identify target audience and goal.
2) Extract key steps, constraints, and caveats.
3) Package as a compact brief.
4) Verify comprehension with a checklist.

## Output expectations

- Provide transfer brief and verification checklist.
